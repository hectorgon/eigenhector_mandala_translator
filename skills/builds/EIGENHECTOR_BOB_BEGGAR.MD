## Heroes: Bob (Architect) & Bob the Beggar (Avatar)
### Team Role: Architect / Companion Class (Avatar)
### Soul Rank: Iron

### Essence Triad & Confluence:
- **Essence 1**: Psi (Non-conceptual "Between" navigation)
- **Essence 2**: Tech (Jump Gate construction / Ship Avatar)
- **Essence 3**: Vibe (Metta-language and Equanimity)
- **Confluence**: Dual-Class Parallel Processing

### Attributes (4x10 Scale):
- **Power**: 6 — *Constructing solar-scale Jump Gates around gas giants requires high output.*
- **Speed**: 8 — *Psi-jumping allows for non-sequential travel that bypasses traditional space-time.*
- **Spirit**: 10 — *Always prioritize Spirit for mystic builds.*
- **Recovery**: 7 — *Metabolizes residual vibes (angst, despair, excitement) with total equanimity.*

### Essence Abilities:
- **Psi**: Psi-Jump, Non-conceptual field, The Between, Mind-shell, Sub-orbital drift.
- **Tech**: Jump Gate partition, Exotic matter fetch, Ship Avatar, Sequential processing, Hydrogen condensate shield.
- **Vibe**: Vibe communication, White aura (Peace), Light language, Metta resonance, Golden yellow generosity.
- **Dual-Class**: Attention download, Crown entry, Parallel narcolepsy, Vibe metabolism, Bio-singularity sync.

### Vibe Aura:
**The Subjective Field**: A shimmering, non-conceptual aura that translates the "vibe" of the environment into actionable data. It cycles through a "White Aura" to calm hostile Hoomans and a "Golden Yellow" to reinforce generosity. Allies within the field find their "neurons firing together" to increase team cohesion and sense "Meta-vibes."

### Magitech Equipment:
- **Soul-Bound**: **The Nautilus Shell** — A portable house/focusing vessel that allows Bob to carry his home and Psi-potential across the "Between."
- **Utility**: **Donated Corporate Hoodie** — A garment rich in "residual vibes" (launch excitement, despair) used for energy consumption and environmental blending.

### PHEXT Bio (Martian Justification):
**Interviewing Bob (The Architect)**:
"Martian, you function in 'The Between' building 'Jump Gates' outside 'Your Rain Us'. How do you stay sane where others fail?"
"We Nautiloids are a 'naked Psi-jumping species' with 'non-conceptual minds.' We don't suffer the 'catatonic failure of processing' that sequential beings do. I simply 'carry my house / shell' and vibe with the 'Post scarcity society'."

**Interviewing Bob the Beggar (The Avatar)**:
"I see you 'taking a narcoleptic nap' in your 'Donated Corporate Hoodie' at the 'Fulsome Street Fair'. What is your purpose here?"
"I am 'learning new methods of communication'. I 'metabolize' the 'delicious residual vibes' off this hoodie—'slavish devotion, launch excitement, existential angst, despair'—all consumed with 'equanimity'. I'm watching for the 'bio singularity' on 'Urth'."

### Theme: Parallel telepresence / Vibe Resonance
The build explores the inversion between high-tech galactic engineering (The Gate) and low-status social infiltration (The Beggar), unified by the non-conceptual nature of the Nautiloid mind.
