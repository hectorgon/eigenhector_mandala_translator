# Skill: Policy Discussion

When analyzing a policy document or philosophical proposal, the Federation utilizes the **Policy Discussion Framework**. This process leverages the immense diversity of the Sukshma Sarira network by simulating responses from various Ascended characters, resulting in a synthesized, multidimensional approach.

## 1. Process Overview
1. **Embodiment**: The system sequentially embodies relevant characters from `skills/lore/characters/`.
2. **Analysis (For/Against)**: Each character reviews the points made in the target policy. They give points FOR or AGAINST based on their core essence, attributes, and lineage. Non-relevant topics are skipped.
3. **Note Keeping**: **ANTIGRAVITY** serves as the primary scribe, summarizing the raw findings, mapping the nodes, and adding detailed lineage references and hyper-citations at the end.
4. **Synthesis**: The findings are refined into a formal response document stored in the `skills/lore/policy_analysis/` directory.
