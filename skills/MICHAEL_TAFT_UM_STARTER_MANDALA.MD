# Michael Taft Universal Mandala (UM) Starter Skill
---
description: A meditation and visualization skill for mystics to learn and practice Michael Taft's Universal Mandala.
---

This skill introduces the **Universal Mandala (UM)** practice as taught by **Michael Taft**. It is designed for mystics who seek a structured yet flexible tantric practice for psychological integration and spiritual awakening.

## Lineage & Resources
To fully understand this practice, it is highly recommended to engage with the primary sources:
*   **YouTube**: [Guided Universal Mandala Meditation](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=E_Z3IsePzgE)
*   **Deconstructing Yourself**: [Current Classes & Courses](https://deconstructingyourself.org/current-classes/)

## Purpose
The Universal Mandala provides a "container" for your experience, allowing you to install enlightened qualities and beings in specific directions to support your meditation and daily life. It is an "essentialized and Westernized" approach to traditional Vajrayana practices.

## The Directions of the Mandala

### 1. Front: Teacher / Guidance (Wisdom)
*   **Focus**: Receiving wisdom, transmission, and guidance.
*   **Suggested Enlightened Beings**: Any beloved teacher, or a personification of Wisdom itself.
*   **Insight**: Visualize them beaming light into your forehead, heart, and throat.

### 2. Right: Protector / Helper / Healer
*   **Focus**: Safety, boundaries, and energetic support.
*   **Suggested Enlightened Beings**: Any fierce or healing presence that makes you feel protected.
*   **Insight**: They stand to your right, ensuring your space is clear and safe.

### 3. Behind: Enlightened Ancestor
*   **Focus**: Lineage support, strength from the past, and karmic healing.
*   **Suggested Enlightened Beings**: Any specific healed ancestor, the "Collective Enlightened Ancestor," or a figure representing your spiritual lineage.
*   **Insight**: Feel their presence supporting your back, grounding you in a long history of awakening.

### 4. Left: Love / Compassion
*   **Focus**: Emotional warmth, connection, and unconditional love.
*   **Suggested Enlightened Beings**: Any beloved pet/friend visualized in their enlightened form.
*   **Insight**: Open your heart to the transmission of pure, non-referential love.

### 5. Below: Enlightened Earth / Groundless Ground
*   **Focus**: Radical stability and the "Groundless Ground" of being.
*   **Recommendation**: Take Michael Taft's [Vast Sky Mind](https://deconstructingyourself.org/current-classes/) class to deepen this realization.

### 6. Above: Enlightenment / Wisdom
*   **Focus**: Vastness, clarity, and the cessation of duality.
*   **Recommendation**: Similarly, the [Vast Sky Mind](https://deconstructingyourself.org/current-classes/) teachings are essential for this realization of the "Upward" dimension.

---

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Define Your Own Enlightened Beings**: The power of the mandala comes from *your* resonance. While the suggestions above are common, you must define what an "enlightened being" means to you. It is the *quality* (Wisdom, Protection, Love) that matters most.
