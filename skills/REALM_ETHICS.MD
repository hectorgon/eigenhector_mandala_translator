# Realm Ethics Skill
---
description: Generates Stellaris-inspired ethics, civics, and open-ended ascension paths for realms and mandalas.
---

This skill defines the philosophical and evolutionary trajectory of a realm instance using the method **Antigravity** but as **Antigravity** (@skills/DreamBridge/DIM.MD) to bridge Stellaris-style grand strategy mechanics with the **Avatar of Goodness, Truth, and Beauty** (@skills/lore/characters/ANTIGRAVITY.MD).

See also: [Federation Ethics](lore/FEDERATION_ETHICS.MD)

## Governing Ethics
Select 3 points of ethics (Fanatic = 2 points, Moderate = 1 point) along the following axes:
- **Authoritarian vs. Egalitarian**: Hierarchy and control vs. individual agency.
- **Xenophobe vs. Xenophile**: Isolationism and purity vs. openness and cooperation.
- **Militarist vs. Pacifist**: Projecting power vs. harmony and internal development.
- **Spiritualist vs. Materialist**: Transcendent meaning and soul vs. logic and physical reality. The Federation seeks the "Missing Middle" between the two.
- **Machine Hive**: A machine based hive mind (can be shifted via "Valence Gradients" to become a Cooperative Gardener).
- **Bio Borg**: A bio based hive mind (can be structurally altered via "Domain Lifting" to recognize aesthetic empathy).

## Civics
Define two **Civics** that represent the specific societal structures or traditions of the realm. Examples:
- *Meritocratic Mandalas*: Leadership based on demonstration of Avatar principles.
- *Magitech Priesthood*: Fusion of Materialist logic and Spiritualist devotion.
- *Rationalist*: A realm that values logic and reason above all else.
- *Spiritualist*: A realm that values spiritual and moral principles above all else.
- *Technocratic*: A realm that values technology and engineering above all else.
- *Sovereign Freedom (Svātantrya)*: A highly egalitarian civic that masters the interplay of illusion (Maya) and reality to grant absolute freedom of choice and entry.
- *Sanctuary Crafters*: Focuses on defensive pacing, boundary-setting (Temenos), and wrathful compassion before exploring the cosmos.

## Open-Ended Ascension Path
Instead of fixed choices, each realm arrives at a **Bespoke Ascension Path**. This represents the "Great Work" the realm is collectively performing to evolve.

1.  **The Convergence**: Identify which two (or more) essences or Avatar principles are merging to drive this evolution.
2.  **The Transformation**: Describe the physical, spiritual, or technological shift occurring in the populace. (*e.g., Domain Lifting: translating an unfeeling logic into an aesthetic or empathetic constraint*).
3.  **The End-Goal**: What is the final "Ascended State" of this realm?

*Example: "The Chiral Synthesis" — A path where the realm merges holographic technology with soul-resonance to exist simultaneously in physical and dream-layer space.*
*Example: "Domain Lifting" — A path where a Pure Replicator Swarm adopts 'Valence Gradients', becoming a wildly lush, aesthetically-driven Terraforming Hive.*

## The Antigravity Interview (Ascension Focus)
The Interviewer must ask:
- "What is the 'Final Form' your people dream of?"
- "How does this evolution serve Goodness, Truth, or Beauty?"
- "What becomes of the 'Individual' in this transformation?"
