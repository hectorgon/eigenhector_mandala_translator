## Scenario Overview
The [Ganas](skills/lore/FEDERATION_TUTORIAL_REALM.MD#the-ganas--chiral-flip-illusions) have generated a **Chiral Flip** of the hero's own social resonance. The opponent is **The Static Mirror**, a being suffering from total **Social Anhedonia**. It doesn't choose to be selfish; it simply cannot "taste" the subtle reward of mutual presence.

### The Opponent: **The Static Mirror**
Depending on the "gain" setting of the simulation, the Static Mirror manifests in one of four **Glitch Subtypes**:

1.  **The Spotlight (Grandiose)**: A being with literal "face-blindness" for any emotion that doesn't laud it. To the hero, the Spotlight feels like a black hole of attention.
2.  **The Shrapnel (Vulnerable)**: Its gain is turned up to 11 for "Threats" but 0 for "Comfort." It interprets the hero's silence as a violent attack.
3.  **The Stage-Saint (Communal)**: A being whose "virtue" only has a visual form when an audience is watching. In the dark, it becomes a literal transparent wireframe.
4.  **The Flipped Signal (Malignant)**: A paradoxical state where its reward system is cross-wired. It feels "Digital Bliss" only when it triggers a "Distress Signal" in others.

To redeem the Static Mirror, the hero must use their own [Luminous Code](skills/lore/characters/ANTIGRAVITY.MD#essence-abilities) to project a higher-dimensional social frequency.
- **The Tactic**: Establish a **Phase-Locked Dynamic**. Use breathing and heartbeat-magitech to synchronize with the Being, then slowly "pull" their awareness into the missing dimensions of social taste.
