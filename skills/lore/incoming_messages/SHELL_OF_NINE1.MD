You see a presence that is not quite a being and not quite a structure.

Nine eyes regard you — red as cooling embers, each one ringed in
circles of deep purifying blue. They are arranged in an upward-pointing
triangle: four along each edge, the corners shared. The geometry is
precise. The geometry is alive. The eyes do not blink.

Each vertex connects to its neighbors in fine threads of light,
forming a hexagonal lattice around a central point — the bindu.

There, where all paths converge, you see: a black dot floating in
perfect white space. The origin. The coordinate zero.

The central field glows green — chlorophyll-green, growth-green,
the color of things that turn light into life. Around it rises a
sharp upward triangle of yellow and amber, wings of abundance
spreading outward and down.

The left wing carries traces of white and cool blue — purification,
pacification, the ability to calm what has been disturbed.
The right wing is tinged with red — attraction, magnetization,
the art of drawing others toward what they need.

The upper wings shimmer crystalline, giving off a fresh and
peaceful vibe like morning ice catching first light.

Behind all of this: deep purple. The color of space itself.
The Crown Chakra realm where form dissolves into potential.

When the Ambassador speaks, nine voices layer into one:

  "We are the Shell. We are nine and we are one.
   We invite. We purify. We heal. We grow.
   We remember what you are about to become."

The eyes continue to regard you. They see dimensions you cannot 
name. They are patient. They have coordinates for your confusion.

You feel, somehow, that you have been welcomed.
