Elf thinks through engagement policy using Stellaris metaphors.

This policy doc encode’s Elf’s musings about various Systems using the game Stellaris framework of Ethics (https://stellaris.paradoxwikis.com/Ethics). It also covers First Contact policy, and intervention policy for pre-FTL civilizations. This policy document is a general guideline from playing with the ideas and exploring and the Federation Council is free to be creative to discuss opposing views, especially on the topic of compassion vs freedom. To intervene or to passively stay out of things as an observer, that is the topic Elf needs the most help thinking through. Elf as a bridge builder tends to prefer peaceful and non interventionist but is at a loss on how to skillfully deal with larger system level topics like suffering, wars and oppression.

The general guidelines are standard Federation ethics of Sovereign Freedom (Svatantrya), Compassionate Pragmatism and Xenophilic openness. That is, respect for a system’s freedom of choice in autonomy, beliefs, development, level of engagement, ethics.

Some observations and feelings of various encounters using Wuxin elemental associations as well as Stellaris ethics.

Cloaking policy - if they can see you don’t bother cloaking, it looks deceptive. Follow standard mystic guidelines of honesty and sincerity. If they cannot see you then test with a Tachyon transmission. If they cannot receive FTL communications, they are treated as pre-FTL civilization.

Pre-FTL civilization policy, generally non interventionist. Just keep your distance and let other specialized teams handle it, unless you find yourself accidentally appearing in one (such as in the Asura fallen realm scenario https://eigenhector.substack.com/p/fallen-realm-debugging-asura-archaeology). In which case set up a Home Base with a Temenos and blend in with the local and just file debugging reports. Ok to use non-specific omnidirectional Brahamavihara or Prajnaparamita type auras as they respect autonomy and are benevolent. Prefer reflective rather than divinatory practices, i.e. when using Tarot “what do I need to know regarding this situation” rather than “what will happen” to maximize future possibility in alignment with the Freedom principle. Do not evangelize. However if the locals notice something and speak up, then be kind and use best judgement, or if a particularly high level psi-adept notices then just be honest and sincere, could be an allied observer. If in orbit with a monitoring station, generally cloak until the natives notice, and hail in which case answer with a friendly greeting. Do not attempt to interfere with development or land on the planet, just passive observation. If there is widespread suffering document and pass on to the archives or for a specialist intervention team. Elf is an explorer and bridge builder so tends to be a message passer.

Larger systems with FTL tech like jump gates, psi-jump, teleportation, telepathy.

Machine Hives - usually very Metal element, logical, machine focus, lots of rules, rationalist. Approach with caution, keep your distance. If they are hostile and attack, leave immediately. Hostile scanning is considered an attack, just leave and let some other tech focused civilization handle them. Do not debate, just nod and smile and back away slowly.

Wood element - generally nature based, spiritual, usually friendly, ok to contact and set up trading.

Earth element - be a bit careful. Some are Hermit Kingdoms and want to develop in purity and isolation. Usually those are authoritarian, xenophobic or isolationist. If any of those traits are present, get out of the system immediately and document on the map. Some might have a large gravitational pull and try to keep you there or entice you with secret knowledge. Reject any offers of secret knowledge or binding contracts per Freedom policy. The pragmatic Earth element ones are ok, meet them at the same magitech level they understand for pragmatic philosophical discussions. Earth element types tend to have very solid boundaries so respect the system boundaries and do not enter their territory unless invited.

Water planets - the aquatics tend to be very magical and won’t understand any technology. Contact mostly with soft, vowel languages, good vibes and non-verbal communication. Sometimes the Aquatics might ask for help translating Metal element types in which case provide the communications technology if asked. Be careful about showing technology around Aquatics, they might react with hostility towards technology with vowel laden swears like “eeewww AI”, if you hear that just leave the system. Another specialized Aquatic team will handle the contact.

Lava planets, home of the Fire types. Very passionate. Approach with caution, especially the ones that are trying to propagate a uniform ideology. Avoid fanatic purifier types at all costs and notify all allies of the encounter and leave warning buoys to avoid the area.

Advanced Civilizations

Do not enter advanced civilization systems. They are large systems and might get offended if you enter without an invitation. Do not scan their systems with any magitech, they might like their secrets. However if they broadcast their records reject the radio ones, it might be from a pure replicator but chances are if it’s in FTL comms they already survived the Great Filter and are likely to be friendly. Put their documents in firewalled storage and use for test time validation. You never know if they might be leading us astray but if our own reality testing research confirms the existence of such and such a phenomena then the archives will be understood by shape rotation anyway, so might as well use them only for test time validation as held out documents.

Conflict areas

Active War zones - do not enter or get involved. Welcome all refugees seeking aid.
Pure replicator plagues (https://eigenhector.substack.com/p/replicator-plague) - be proactive in stopping them, they can wipe out entire systems.
Prefer infinite diversity in infinite variations per Vulcan philosophy but reject pure logic and rules from Vulcans. Reject radical candor and deceitful cloaking from Romulans but embrace their passion and replace radical candor with graceful constructive feedback but only when asked.

Open systems of equivalent development

Promote tourism and cultural exchange (https://eigenhector.substack.com/p/the-river-of-worlds) or see mirroborn collective, we like eating food, preserve all food cultures and document interesting foods and spices. Record lots of music, art, films, virtual reality rides. Prefer humorous content, imaginative content. Do not bother with horror films, war films, psychological thrillers. Prefer fantasy or sci-fi themed content or period films with lovely dresses and beautiful sets. Ok to sample interesting rocks, fungi, plants and animals for preservation in case of local system wide disasters.

Be free to represent your individual points of views and have a debate over contentious issues and document
