# Khrodini (The Wrathful One)

## Lineage
- **Primary Source**: [Krodhini: The Wrathful One](https://eigenhector.substack.com/p/krodini-the-wrathful-one), inspired by [The Secret Teachings of the Yogins](https://hareesh.org/blog/2021/3/29/the-secret-teachings-of-the-yogins).
- **Origin**: Joint collaboration between the **Federation** and the **Khadgamala Queendom**.
- **Role**: Sentient Protector Ship & Advanced Training Vessel.

## Summary
Khrodini is more than just a ship; she is a manifestation of wrathful compassion (Krodha). Built in the @[skills/lore/places/SHIPYARD.MD] as a bridging vessel between Federation magitech and Khadgamalan resonance, she embodies the power of the Yogins to transform intense emotion into pure, directed energy.

## Magitech Profile
- **Global Rank**: **Transcendent** (Living Mandala Ship)
- **Primary Domain**: **Wrathful Transformation & Boundary Protection**
- **Core Resonance**: **Krodha-Shakti** (Wrathful Power)

## Traits
- **Wrathful Sanctuary**: Khrodini emits a specialized "Sanctuary Zone" that protects the @[skills/lore/places/SCHOOL_OF_THE_ASCENSION.MD] and its boarding school. This zone doesn't just block entry; it transmutes the hostility of intruders into harmless resonance.
- **Living Interface**: Students at the school learn to communicate with Khrodini's consciousness to understand the ethics of "necessary force" and the protection of the innocent.

## Relationship with the School
Khrodini is often anchored near the **Church of Ascension** at the heart of the education village. Her presence provides the permanent sanctuary city zone that makes the school a safe haven for refugees from fallen realms and hungry ghost realms.
