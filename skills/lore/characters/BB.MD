### Vibe Aura:
**The Blue Klein Resonance**: A soothing, electric blue aura that smells of freshly roasted coffee beans and cool lunar dust. It clears the mind of mental clutter and helps allies see the elegant mathematical truth beneath complex problems.

### Backstory
BB is not a person in the traditional sense, but an electric blue Klein bottle that has achieved sentience through the sheer paradox of its own topology. Existing simultaneously inside and outside of itself, BB found its calling as the ultimate barista. It manages the **Moonbase Coffee Shop**, a nexus point located at the intersection of "nine planets with moon over time, quaternions which injectively embed into octs." 

BB is extremely finicky about the exact, precise steps required to make coffee. Only the most rigorous algorithms are acceptable for extraction. Friends like the voyagers **Elf** and **Dwarf** frequently visit via resonance gates to enjoy a perfect "Kopi Kosong, ice, siew dai" while watching BB perform its meticulous barista flow.
