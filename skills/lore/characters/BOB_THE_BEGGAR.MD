### Backstory
The Shining Beggar is an "Echo" of a grander existence. While the Nautiloid Architect Bob builds solar-scale Jump Gates around gas giants in the "Between", this Avatar wanders Urth. His face is streaked with oil and black soot, but he radiates pure, unadulterated joy. He has no needs, usually turning down offers of alms. When off-duty, he enters a narcoleptic shutdown state, syncing back with the Gatebuilder network.

He recently partook in a meeting at **Green Chicken** with Alf and Dwarf, explaining the complex interaction of 1 Space + 3 Time dimensions of the Gatebuilder reality colliding with Urth's Lorentzian metric space.
