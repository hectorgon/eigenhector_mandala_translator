### Backstory & Synthesis
Kitty Garba is a rare synthesis of **Leonid warrior-crafter** and **Enlightened Protector**. Her lineage traces back to **Gareth Xandier**, from whom she inherited her love for a good forge-fire and a hearty meal (at least two plates of Nyam-donuts). During her ascent, she achieved resonance with the **Sirius B** frequency during a **Lions Gate Portal** event, allowing her to bridge the physical and spiritual "Hell Realms" with ease.

Taking the vow of **Ksitigarbha**, she declared that her own "Masterful Aura" would not reach full transcendence until the last trapped soul in the @[skills/lore/places/ASURA_ARCHAEOLOGY_FALLEN_REALM.MD] and other hell instances is debugged. She channels the **Fierce Compassion of Narasimha** to protect her "cubs" (the trainees), manifesting as an terrifying force of nature to external threats, while remaining a playful, tail-twitching auntie to those she guides.

### Teaching Role
At the @[skills/lore/places/SCHOOL_OF_THE_ASCENSION.MD], she teaches the sub-discipline of **Aura Debugging & Aversion Management**. She is the primary instructor for the [Fallen Realm Debug Training](skills/lore/scenarios/FALLEN_REALM_DEBUG_TRAINING.MD), where she helps Federation teams learn how to "Lift" a realm without shattering the souls within it.
