### Vibe Aura:
**The Love-Fire**: A warm, amber glow that smells of freshly baked bread, saffron, and aged wine. It calms the heart and makes even the most parched teams feel "juicy" with life energy.

### Resonance Protocols:
- **The Glitching Patron Protocol**: Rumi maintains a special corner for those "seizing" across realms. He provides a stable anchor for their momentary appearances, treating them with the same kindness as a king.

### Backstory
Rumi appeared at the base of the **Inn of All Worlds** as if he had always been there. Behind the long wooden counter of his restaurant, he serves food that feeds the soul. He is a dark-skinned man in white robes with a white turban, radiating a kindness that transcends time. 

He once explained to the voyager **Elf** that the man appearing and disappearing at the bar was simply having a seizure in another realm—in Rumi's restaurant, such occurrences are treated with grace, as every arrival is a guest sent from beyond.
