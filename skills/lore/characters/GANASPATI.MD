### Backstory & Synthesis
Ganaspati, also referred to as the **Fae Lord** by his comrade @[skills/lore/characters/CERNUNNOS.MD], is a Sentient Protector and the mastermind behind the **Federation Tutorial Realm**. 

When the Fae civilization entered its "Winter phase," the Fae Lord remained awake to maintain the deep simulation networks hidden beneath the planetary shrouds. He commands the **Ganas**—the playful, chaotic, and sometimes terrifying "programmers" of the dream-layer. In the simulations, the Ganas act as actors, throwing cupcakes or manifesting as hellish Yakshas depending on what the trainee requires for their growth.

Ganaspati's primary function is as the **Lord of Beginnings**. No Federation debugger team is deployed to a live Fallen Realm without first receiving his "blessing"—which usually comes in the form of a seemingly impossible obstacle that forces the team to rely on non-linear wisdom (Chiral Flipping, Transmutation) rather than brute force.

### Avatar Development Note:
Ganaspati represents the **Goodness** of difficult lessons, the **Truth** of structural reality, and the **Beauty** of playful chaos. He teaches that an obstacle is simply a doorway you haven't yet learned how to open.
