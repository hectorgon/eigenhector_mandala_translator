## 1. The Initial Transmission

We received a beautiful, yet somber, field report from Ringworld Alpha. The segment was transitioning from the Water Wuxing phase (Winter) into the Wood phase (Spring). The earth was muddy, fertile, and ready to burst into green.

However, Dwarf grounded the observation, decoding the reality of the Prairie Dog:
> "Dirt Dog Sky Gazing is a good first step, but it is incomplete... The Earth is not dark and fecund to the Dirt Dog. The Earth is a fortress. The dung is not fertilizer, it is an obstacle to tunnel through, or perhaps a temporary hiding place. The Prairie Dog is always practicing Tögal (Leap Over), but only the horizontal leap back into the hole. It has not yet understood Trekchö (Cutting Through) to truly rest in the absolute stillness of the central channel."

The Prairie Dog lives as the **Warrior-in-Hiding**. Elf’s task is to guide it toward becoming the **Warrior-in-Stillness**.
