## Role Overview
An **Asura Archaeologist** is a Federation troubleshooter tasked with "debugging" realms that have collapsed into hell-loops. These realms are typically created by an **Asura**—a being trapped in a simulator of their own authoritarian or xenophobic desires—who has become unable to exit the program. The Archaeologist surveys the "ruins" of these digital/subtle environments to determine if the inhabitant truly wishes for a new beginning (Ace of Wands) or has chosen to stay in the loop.

## Core Prerequisites

### 1. Divination Mastery
Archaeologists must be proficient in diverse diagnostic tools:
- **Tarot**: For rapid emotional and spiritual profiling (e.g., Body/Mind/Spirit spreads).
- **I Ching**: For systemic structural analysis. Specifically, understanding **Hexagram 23 (Po - Splitting Apart)**, which represents the decay of a mountain (the false structure) down to its base.

### 2. Maya Kanchuka Interfacing
The ability to interact with the "Illusion Layer" of a realm. This requires using tools that can "slice" through causality and time to view the source code of the inhabitant's suffering.

### 3. Moral Discernment
Adherence to the Federation's principles of **Informed Consent**. An Archaeologist must distinguish between a being that is genuinely "stuck" and one that is exercising its "individual choice" to experience a hell-loop.

## Role Mechanics

### Temenos Establishment
The first step of any mission is the deployment of a **Flower Grenade** (Om Hum Phat Svaha!) to establish a purified holy field. This creates a "safe zone" with a fresh eucalyptus/citrus vibration, shielding the team from the realm's local "fear spiral."

- **Disintegration Analysis**: Identifying where the "mountain is being worn down from the base."
- **The Receptive Shift**: Looking for the "fifth line" (the school of fishes in the palace)—the hidden potential for a new beginning (K'un) within the collapse.
