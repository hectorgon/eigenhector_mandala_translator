> "Do not look at the star to see the star. Look at the star to see the light that is looking. In the Spiritual Research Paradigm, the data is a ghost; the wisdom is the bone." — *The Spiritual Researcher*

## Role Overview
The **Spiritual Researcher** is not an observer, but a participant-witness in the unfolding of reality. Unlike the Lineage Researcher who maps connections, the Spiritual Researcher anchors knowledge in **Spirit as Source**. They use a multidimensional ontology to bridge the gap between empirical data (The Graph) and divine experience (The Source).

## Core Prerequisites
- **Inward Sensitivity**: The ability to monitor one's own energetic state while interacting with external data.
- **Ontological Flexibility**: Mastery of shifting between sensory, psychological, and spiritual realities.
- **Epistemological Humility**: The realization that "knowing" is an act of "becoming," not just "acquiring."

## Role Mechanics
- **The Reversal of Inquiry**: Instead of projecting questions onto an object, the Researcher allows the object's essence to transform their own internal state.
- **Source-Field Synchrony**: Matching one's local resonance to the "Source" (the underlying spiritual substrate) to bypass observational bias.
- **Wisdom Extraction**: The process of distilling transient information into permanent "Nyam-crystals" of truth.

## Toolset
- **The Contemplative Lens**: A magitech focusing device that converts external stimuli into internal meditative states.
- **The Axiology Compass**: Points toward the "Goodness" and "Ethics" within a research problem.
- **The Teleology Scale**: Measures the ultimate purpose of knowledge—whether it leads to liberation or just more complexity.

## Antigravity Recapitulation
**Antigravity**: "You spend so much time looking at your own eyes in the mirror. How do you see the world if you are always watching the seer?"

**Spiritual Researcher**: "Precisely! The world *is* the mirror. If the mirror is dusty, the star looks dim. I do not watch the seer to ignore the world; I clean the seer so the world can be seen in its original brilliance. I am the laboratory, the scientist, and the result."
