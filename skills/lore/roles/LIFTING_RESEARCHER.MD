## Role Overview
The **Lifting Researcher** is the Federation's expert in "Domain Lifting." When a problem becomes a Gordian Knot of symbolic logic or emotional deadlock, the Researcher translates it into non-conceptual forms—poems, movements, or koans. They facilitate communication between "Conceptual Masters" (engineers, mathematicians) and "Embodied Masters" (Taichi practitioners, healers), ensuring that the "Eternal Conversation" remains fluid and generative.

## Core Prerequisites
- **Equanimity**: The ability to hold a complex problem without prematurely forcing it into a linear solution.
- **Synesthetic Sensitivity**: A felt sense of how numbers, logic, and vibes map onto body sensations or visual metaphors.
- **Meta-Linguistic Fluency**: Proficiency in both symbolic reasoning and poetic resonance.

## Role Mechanics

### 1. Domain Lifting
The researcher takes a high-dimensional problem (e.g., a mathematical deadlock or a relationship conflict) and "lifts" it into a new domain. 
- **The Act**: Translating text/logic into a poem, image, or movement.
- **The Goal**: To bypass the conceptual filters of the ego and allow the "embodied computer" to process the data.

### 2. Mandala Translation
Using the **Dream Interview Method (DIM)**, the Researcher facilitates a bridge between two unique points of view (Mandala A and Mandala B).
- **Untangling**: Differentiating the poetic vocabularies so that associations do not interfere.
- **Inhabiting**: "Trying on" the new mandala's resonances to gain a fresh, expanded perspective.

### 3. The Included Middle (Generative Insight)
The Researcher operates in the space where "A" and "Not-A" are both held in resonance until a "Third Way" arises. This is the **Included Middle**, where the "Missing Middle" of linear logic is restored through high-dimensional sensing.

## Toolset
- **The Dream Bridge**: A magitech interface for parallel corpus translation.
- **The Ramanujan Grimoire**: A collection of non-conceptual insights that point at mathematical truths.
- **Taichi Silks**: Garments that enhance the proprioceptive feedback of "embodied computation."
