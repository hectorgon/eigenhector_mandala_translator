# Spell Effects: The Mandala of Color and Intent
---
description: A guide for heroes to understand the color associations and energetic foundations of spell effects within the Federation.
---

In the Federation, magic and technology are not merely tools but expressions of specific energetic frequencies. To master the use of spell effects, a hero must understand the color symbology that governs the interaction between intent, element, and activity.

## 1. The Four Tantric Activities (Caturkarman)
The most fundamental layer of spell design is the **Intent**. Why are you casting? What is the goal of the energy?

| Activity | Color | Effect Type | Principle |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Pacification** (*śāntika*) | **White** | Healing, Cleansing, Shielding | Removing obstacles, soothing pain, and restoring equanimity. |
| **Enriching** (*pauṣṭika*) | **Yellow** | Buffs, Resource Gain, Growth | Increasing wealth, merit, lifespan, and positive attributes. |
| **Magnetization** (*vaśīkaraṇa*) | **Red** | Taunts, Pulls, Influence | Attracting allies, drawing in enemies, and exerting charismatic power. |
| **Subjugation** (*abhicāra*) | **Black/Dark Blue** | CC, Forced Control, Execution | Subduing hostile forces and dealing lethal or binding damage. |

## 2. The Five Divine Families
For more specialized builds, effects are flavored by the wisdom and element of a specific Buddha or Tara family.

### The Buddha Families (The Wisdoms)
- **Vairochana (White)**: **Space**. Effects that expand awareness or clear the field of delusions. 
    - *Example*: [EIGENHECTOR_DIVINE_ARMOR](skills/builds/EIGENHECTOR_DIVINE_ARMOR.MD) (White radiance of non-duality).
- **Akshobhya (Blue)**: **Water**. Effects that provide mirror-like clarity and unshakable stability.
    - *Example*: [ARI_SKY_INVERTER](skills/builds/ARI_SKY_INVERTER.MD) (Blue void of the Outer Sky).
- **Ratnasambhava (Yellow)**: **Earth**. Effects that create abundance and recognize the equality of all beings.
    - *Example*: [EIGENHECTOR_PIGSY](skills/builds/EIGENHECTOR_PIGSY.MD) (Golden nectar of Dana).
- **Amitabha (Red)**: **Fire**. Effects that transmute desire into discriminating wisdom and compassionate action.
    - *Example*: [EIGENHECTOR_BURNING_BONES](skills/builds/EIGENHECTOR_BURNING_BONES.MD) (Sacred Fire of purification).
- **Amoghasiddhi (Green)**: **Air**. Effects that accomplish all virtuous goals with swift, unobstructed speed.
    - *Example*: [EIGENHECTOR_ELF_DWARF](skills/builds/EIGENHECTOR_ELF_DWARF.MD) (Vivid green qi and wind-moving leaves).

### The Tara Families (The Activities)
- **White Tara**: Longevity and Healing.
- **Green Tara**: Protection and Swift Action.
- **Blue Tara (Ekajati)**: Wrathful Wisdom and obstacle removal.
- **Yellow Tara**: Prosperity and Merit increase.
- **Red Tara (Kurukulla)**: Magnetization and Love.

## 3. The Chakra Elemental System
When dealing with raw elemental forces or physical combat effects, use the classic chakra color ladder.

- **Root (Red)**: **Earth**. Grounding strikes, physical resistance, and survival instincts.
- **Sacral (Orange)**: **Water**. Fluidity, creativity, and emotional ripples.
- **Solar Plexus (Yellow)**: **Fire**. Personal power, confidence, and transformative heat.
- **Heart (Green)**: **Air**. Compassion, breath-control, and movement speed.
- **Throat (Blue)**: **Space/Ether**. Communication, truth-echoes, and vibratory resonance.
- **Third Eye (Indigo/Violet)**: **Light**. Intuition, 11D navigation, and psychic sight.
- **Crown (White/Gold)**: **Spirit**. Unity, enlightenment, and divine descent.

## 4. Spell Visual Guidelines

### Combination Effects
When a spell combines intent and element, its visual should blend the colors:
- **A Healing Fire spell** (Pacification + Fire): Shimmers with **Iridescent White and Orange-Red**.
- **A Magnetizing Earth spell** (Magnetization + Earth): Glows with **Deep Red and Dark Browns**.
- **An Enriching Air spell** (Enriching + Air): Appears as **Golden-Green mists**.
