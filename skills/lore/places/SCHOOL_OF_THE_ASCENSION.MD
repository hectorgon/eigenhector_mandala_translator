# School of the Ascension

## Lineage
- **Primary Source**: Elf's childhood memories, [The Ramen and the Chicken Hero](https://eigenhector.substack.com/p/the-ramen-and-the-chicken-hero)
- **Location**: Core of the education village, @[skills/lore/places/SHIPYARD.MD].
- **Founding Collaboration**: Federation and Khadgamala Queendom.

## Summary
The **School of the Ascension** is a premier educational facility and refuge located within the **Sukshma Sarira Shipyards**. It serves as the primary training ground for Federation fallen realm debuggers and a sanctuary for children rescued from hungry ghost realms and war zones. 

The school is centered around the **Ascension Cathedral**, a magitech structure that emits a permanent "Sanctuary City Zone" for protection, maintained by the Living Ship @[skills/lore/characters/KHRODINI.MD]. Folks go there to sing beautiful songs and commune with the divine.

## Architecture & Aesthetics
- **Salmon Pink Walls**: The school is distinctively salmon pink, featuring **upturned fish scale texturing** on the walls. 
    - *I Ching Metaphor*: The fish scale texture evokes the hexagram **Zhong Fu** (Inner Truth), where the "pig and fish" are reached by the influence of truth. It also references the carp leaping over the Dragon Gate—a symbol of educational transformation and perseverance.
- **The Magitech Bell Tower**: A tall, slender tower that houses a bell signifying **Emptiness** (Shunyata). When struck, the bell doesn't just sound; it clears the local "static" of the Sukshma Sarira, allowing students to perceive the formless sea of reality behind the illusions of space and time.
- **The Long Stairs**: A series of stairs leading from the canteen down to the green field, representing the descent from nourishment to application.

## Infrastructure
- **The Canteen**: Offers free food for all students and refugees, managed by @[skills/lore/characters/AUNTIE_GUAN_YIN.MD] and @[skills/lore/characters/YEE_RANG_PAU.MD]. It is a place of radical kindness and community.
- **The Boarding School**: A safe haven for children rescued from fallen realms. It is run by Auntie Guan Yin and Yee Rang Pau, who provide both parental care and foundational education.
- **The Green Field (Tutorial Realm Simulator)**: Located at the bottom of the hill. As trainees step onto the field, it renders into the @[skills/lore/FEDERATION_TUTORIAL_REALM.MD] instance for high-fidelity simulation training.
