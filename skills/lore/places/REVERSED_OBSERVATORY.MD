## Summary
The **Reversed Observatory** is a unique research facility located in the upper atmosphere of **Ringworld Alpha**, hovering near the **Sukshma Sarira Shipyards**. Built by the **Aarti Jaganmatri** initiative, it serves as the home and laboratory for **Arti Jagan-Lin** and other practitioners of the Spiritual Research Paradigm.

The architecture is a series of nested glass spheres that pulse with violet and gold light. Instead of massive arrays searching the deep Transcend for physical matter, the Observatory features "Inward Arrays"—crystalline structures that map the multidimensional layers of the human spiritual experience.
