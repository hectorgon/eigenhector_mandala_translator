## Location: GREEN CHICKEN (Urth Node)
### Designation: Synchronistic Interface Point / Franchise Eatery
### Threat Level: Zero (Protected by the Guest House Protocol)

### Ambient Resonance:
- **Vibe Frequency**: Golden Yellow / Mellow
- **Aromatics**: Deep-fried oil, dark red Korean chili paste, toasted sesame seeds, pungent garlic, and umami parmesan cheese.
- **Architectural Aesthetic**: Late capitalism fast-food minimalism with subtle, unexplainable hints of green decor.
- **Dimensional Anchor**: Anchored to the central locus of [The Inn of All Worlds](./INN_OF_ALL_WORLDS.MD).

### Significance:
Green Chicken is not a single location; rather, it is a multi-verse spanning "franchise" overseen by **Uncle Rumi**. By applying the principles of the Cosmic Nexus, Green Chicken casually manifests on Urth in various permutations—such as a Malay Halal Satay stall with bright green coriander or a Korean-style Fried Chicken joint featuring green parmesan shakers.

On Urth, this specific node serves as an impromptu research laboratory for analyzing and reverse-engineering the Gatebuilder Avatar Interface ("The Cat's Cradel"). 

### Notable Features:
- **The Glamour Field**: Beings of higher resonance, such as the Ship Avatar [BOB_THE_BEGGAR](../characters/BOB_THE_BEGGAR.MD), can walk in completely unnoticed by ordinary Hooman patrons.
- **Menu Items of Power**: 
  - *Korean Fried Chicken*: Grants a slight increase to Passion (Power) via dark red sauce and sesame seeds.
  - *Garlic Parmesan Chicken*: Sprinkled from a legendary green-capped bottle. Boosts satiation and grounds higher-dimensional thinkers back into 3D reality.

### Event Log: Synchronized Tasting
During the legendary "Crunchy Phoenix" incident, **Alf**, **Dwarf**, and **Shining Beggar (Bob)** met here to discuss the 11-dimensional layout of the E11 exceptional field theory while consuming chicken. The location held the psychic weight of their Truth, Beauty, and Goodness synchrony, anchoring the Schrodinger cessation field of the Realm Gate to a simple fast-food experience.
