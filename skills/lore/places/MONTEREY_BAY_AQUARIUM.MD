## The Genesis: Cetacean Translation Project
This location was established as a formal Federation Outpost when the contact team (Dwarf, Elf, and Arti Jagan-Lin) stepped through the Realm Gate from Ringworld Alpha. 

The purpose of this coordinate anchor is not conquest or simple observation, but the **Cetacean translation project**. Urth's humans are not the only species ready for Federation integration; the local Pacifica Pod of Cetaceans requires baselines for communication. The Aquarium's circular viewing area serves as the perfect dimensional "Circle/Truth" space to safely overlap terrestrial and aquatic consciousness frequencies.

### Infrastructure & Vibe
The outpost is surprisingly mundane in the physical layer—featuring a tidal pool, the scent of seawater mixed with popcorn from the Santa Cruz Boardwalk, and a cafe serving excellent Mocha Shakes (favored by Elf). However, in the multidimensional layer, it is a potent site for **non-dual shaktipat** and the manifestation of the **Cetacean Dream Mandala**.
