## Summary
The **River of Worlds** is a profound topological expression of interconnectedness within the greater Cosmos. It is not a single geographical feature, but a functional magic spell—a protocol of transit—whereby any moving body of water (from a humble stream to a Great Ocean) may be invoked as a gateway. 

As observed by the travelers **Elf** and **Dwarf**, a single touch of the finger to the water triggers a "Flip" across Jhanic manifolds, instantly relocating the travelers to disparate Realm Instances. This phenomenon exemplifies the non-dual nature of the Cosmos, where distance is merely a variable in a graph-logic equation.

    - **River of Worlds Invocation**: A simple yet powerful spell that turns water into a mirror of distant worlds.
    - **Vibe Filtering**: The River often deposits travelers in locations that resonate with their current internal state (e.g., a "bougie restaurant" vs. a comforting "hawker center").

## Unique Materials & Artefacts
- **Memetic Satay**: A delicacy found in various Cosmic Hawker Centers. It carries the "Nyam" of local cultures, particularly the green-stained mutton fat of the Nyonya style, which vibrates with coriander resonance.
- **Satay Skewer (Wooden)**: Often discarded after use, yet these simple objects carry the traces of a thousand different worlds encountered along the River.

## Inhabitants & Past Lives
- **Key Entities**: 
    - **The Past Life Debuggers**: High-rank specialists encountered at the Hawker Centers. They specialize in tracing the lineage of Realm Instances, creating **Jataka Tales** to heal inhabitants of past-life trauma.
    - **Elf & Dwarf**: Noted travelers and foodie-mystics who use the River to seek out exotic culinary resonances.
- **Avatar Potential**: This phenomenon represents the **Beauty** of discovery and the **Goodness** of cross-cultural nourishment (both physical and spiritual).

## Ethics & Dharma-Sim (The Ethics of the Transit)
- **Primary Theme**: **Hospitality & Discernment**
- **Ethical Dilemma**: The "Judgment of the Black-Eyed." Travelers may arrive in realms where they are unwelcome or judged (the Bougie Restaurant). The ethical choice is not to force Integration, but to gracefully "Flip" elsewhere until a resonant center (the Hawker Center) is found.
- **Chiral Flip Potential**: **The Dry Path**, a realm where all water is stagnant and transit is impossible, leading to spiritual and cultural isolation.
