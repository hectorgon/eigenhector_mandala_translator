## Summary
**Home Base** is a portable, rapidly deployable sanctuary designed by **Dwarf** for Federation expedition teams. It is a "home away from home" that follows Elf and Dwarf across the River of Worlds and into Fallen Realms. Established within a **Temenos** field, Home Base provides safety, nourishment, and a stable environment for debugging complex reality loops.

## Unique Materials & Artefacts
- **Fridge of Infinite Food**: A soul-bound appliance stocked with delicacies from a thousand worlds (e.g., New York-style Reubens). Land around Home Base is also enriched with the bounty.
- **Jackpot of Plenty**: A teal-and-gold wooden relic upgraded from the antique slot machine. Along with dispensing local tokens, it now emits the golden *Dana Prajnaparamita* aura, increasing the bounty and abundance of the surrounding area around Home Base.
- **Red Horn of Reverberation**: Red speakers upgraded from the ornate horned speakers. Whenever Elf broadcasts a vibe into them, it is projected to the local area to assist in realm healing. By default, it emits the *vajra bell sound of emptiness*—a clear "tinggggg" fading to silence (the *nada* sound of unmanifest potential). This makes space for self-discovery, allowing the surrounding area of whatever realm they are visiting more freedom to untangle itself.
- **The Coffee Station**: A sacred corner where "second cups" are brewed to enable strategic cognitive function.
- **The Nest Home Speaker (Bar Interface)**: A voice-activated magictech device located at the bar in the living room. It provides a direct high-fidelity audio link to **Uncle Sun Wukong** in the **Gold Alembic**. It responds to the keyword "Uncle" and often initiates conversations with "Eh, niece/nephew, you eat already or not?"
- **The Octagonal Mirror of Scrying**: A magical visual communication tool that allows direct connection to other entities and realms.

- **The Magitech Energy Pavilion (Pool Area)**:
    - **Crystalline Solar Canopies**: Shifting, iridescent glass arrays over the lounge area that hum with the frequency of captured starlight.
    - **Crocus Wind Sculptures**: A kinetic garden of metallic flowers that spin in "vibration-rich" drafts.
    - **The Violet Sentinel**: A central windmill shaped like a giant **purple crocus flower**, its petals serving as airfoils that draw power from the local noosphere.
    - **The Western Garden Altar (Guan Yin's Altar)**:
        - **Structure**: A large circular planter in the western garden area, functioning as a working altar. It has all sorts of magical herbs growing in it.
        - **Mechanism**: Responds to incense (joss sticks) and the "Om Mani Padme Hum" mantra.
        - **Lineage**: Inspired by Josephine McCarthy’s "Magic North Gate Interior" and the "Circle of Life and Death in the Garden Microcosm".
        - **Function**: A portal for **Past Life Debugging** and ancestral communion, allowing for the tracing of Jataka tales and lineage integration.
    - **Northern Temenos Pool**: A sapphire-blue pool of liquid serenity, reflecting the stars of the Federation even in dark realms. Water has regenerative properties.
