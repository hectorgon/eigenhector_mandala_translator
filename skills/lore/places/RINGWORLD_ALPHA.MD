## Summary
*Record entry compiled by ANTIGRAVITY. Citation lattice stabilized.*

**Ringworld Alpha** is the crowning architectural and spiritual achievement of the current cycle, serving as the majestic capital of the Federation. Originally a ruined megastructure from a bygone era of conflict, it was painstakingly rebuilt as a magitech sanctuary zone through a historic collaboration between two enlightened progenitor races: the **Cybrex** (masters of physical megastructure engineering and synthetic harmony) and the transcendent **Zroni** (weavers of the psionic underlying manifold). It is a place where physical scale and spiritual depth coexist in perfect, macroscopic symmetry.

## Key Facilities & Points of Interest
- **@[skills/lore/places/SHIPYARD.MD]**: The great industrial and educational hub rests along a massive equatorial band of the ringworld, assembling Chakra-class escorts and Mahameru-class vessels while hosting the Tutorial Realm.
- **The Sanctuary Zones**: Vast, curated ecosystems where endangered species and displaced populations from Fallen Realms find safety, reflecting the Federation's commitment to the preservation of life.
- **The Capital Spire (The Great Synthesis)**: A colossal, glowing spire of Meru-grade silicon that reaches toward the central star, housing the Federation's central diplomatic council.

## Inhabitants & Past Lives
- **The Enlightened Cybrex**: Custodians of the physical lattice, maintaining the intricate machinery with gentle precision.
- **The Zroni Echoes**: Luminous, psionic guides who teach novices how to navigate the deeper resonance protocols.
- **Current Dockings**: @[skills/lore/places/HOME_BASE.MD] docks here extensively between high-intensity realm-debugging missions. The tranquil, high-fidelity environment of the sanctuary allows the team (Elf, Dwarf, and their companions) to recharge their 'Catharsis energy' without interference.
