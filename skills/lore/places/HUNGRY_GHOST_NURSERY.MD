## Summary
Contrary to the terrifying traditional descriptions of skeletal beings with needle-thin necks, the **Hungry Ghost Realm** is re-contextualized as a **Nursery for Larval Devas**. While the inhabitants represent **dissatisfaction** (*dukkha*)—including fat ghosts who cannot move and thin ones with bulging eyes—their "hunger" is viewed as the developmental stage of growing light beings.

The realm is a metaphorical dreamscape that exists at the "Shushumna" of the cosmos, an infinite pillar of fire. The **Avatar of Satisfaction** (Pigsy) visits this realm during "feeding time" to transmute dissatisfaction into fulfillment using the **Neijingtu transport algorithm** and elemental sequences.
