## Summary
The **Black Lands** are a metaphysical and vision-based realm centered around the **Cremation Grounds**. It is a place of radical purification where the "Done" state of a corpse (*shavasana*) is used as a foundation for elemental transmutation. Typical inhabitants include Jackals (**Yakshas**), Sadhus, and Ghosts.

The core practice here is the **Burning Bones Bhavana**, where the practitioner visualizes their own body being consumed by sacred fire—purifying Earth (cares/woes), Water (emotions), Fire (inner heat), and Wind (light-body) until only the "might of light" remains in the bones. This realm is a portal to the land of the **Djinn** and serves as a site for guiding ghosts toward liberation.

- **Primary Theme**: **Necessary Violence vs. Universal Destruction (Purification)**

- **Chiral Flip Potential**: A "Cursed Charnel Ground" that burns only for destruction, leaving no light or space, just ashes and despair.
