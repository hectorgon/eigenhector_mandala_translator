## Summary
The **Fallen Realm** investigated by Elf and Dwarf is a decaying simulation instance characterized by "junk yard" aesthetics—filled with rusty equipment, broken fridges, and old tires. These realms are typically created by **Asuras** (beings of intense desire or pride) who program a simulator to fulfill a specific desire (e.g., supreme rule) but eventually get stuck in an isolationist or xenophobic feedback loop.

Debugging these realms involves setting up a **Temenos** (purified field) and a **Home Base** using template-driven construction. Federation teams use tools like the **Time Slicer** to interface with the realm's **Maya Kanchuka** (Illusion Layer) and diagnostic methods like I Ching or Tarot to determine if the inhabitant is ready for "informed consent" to exit their self-created loop.
