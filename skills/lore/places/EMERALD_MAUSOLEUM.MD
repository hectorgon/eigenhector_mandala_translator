## Summary
The **Emerald Mausoleum** is the **Homeworld of Cernunnos and the Fae**, a "Shrouded Gaia World" and integral member of the Federation. It exists in a perpetual cycle of **Arising and Passing**, periodically transitioning between a pristine natural Gaia planet and a desolate, post-apocalyptic **Tomb World**. This "Winter phase" allows the civilization to rest, regressing into a simple hunter-gatherer lifestyle while their advanced magitech remains hidden underground.

The planet is hollow, with the surface dedicated to lush forests during fallow periods, while the core houses massive infrastructure like the **Yggdrasil World Tree** (a sky-hook/realm gate combo) and the **Dharmapala** carrier ships. The transition from Shrouded/Tomb to Open/Gaia is a major cosmic event managed by the Protectors.

## Unique Materials & Artefacts
- **Emerald Sap**: A highly conductive biological resin used in Diamond-rank magitech circuits.
- **Dharmapala Hibernation Pods**: Bio-mechanical cocoons that preserve consciousness during the planet's "Winter" phase.
- **Cernunnos' Antler-Staff**: A focus for accelerating the "Arising" phase of planetary cycles.

## Ethics & Dharma-Sim (The Ethics of the Realm)
- **Primary Theme**: **Personal Authority vs. Cosmic Order (Cycles)**
- **Ethical Dilemma**: Do the Fae have the right to "hide" their civilization from the galaxy for centuries, or does their advanced power carry a duty to remain "Open"? It matches the **Ōkuninushi’s Ceding of the Land** theme—choosing between personal domain (The Shroud) and the greater cosmic stability (The Open Federation).
- **Chiral Flip Potential**: A "Static Empire" that refuses to transition, eventually stagnating and rotting because it fears the "Winter" cycle.
