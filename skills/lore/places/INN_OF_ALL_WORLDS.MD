## The Planet: Chiral Flip
The Inn sits upon a dusty, desolate planet that exists in a state of **Chiral Flip** to the life within the structure. 
- **The Atmosphere**: Thick with yellow dust that parches the throat.
- **The Flora**: Dry farms surrounding the structure, inhabited by "Dandelion Seed" trees—black, spindly trunks topped with white, fluffy seeds that never seem to sprout.
- **The Vibe**: Dry, hungry, parched, and parched-white. It is a world of starvation and isolation.

## The Structure: The Spiral Square
Rising from the yellow dust is a magnificent wooden structure mounted on massive stilts.
- **The Stairs**: Four dark wooden staircases spiral alongside the square sides of the stilts, forming a "Square Spiral" that leads visitors upward toward the sanctuary.
- **The Architecture**: A blend of ancient Earth-inspired woodwork and advanced magitech structural integrity.

## The Foundation: Rumi's Restaurant
At the very bottom floor of the Inn lies the **Cosmic Nexus** known as Rumi's Restaurant.
- **The Counter**: A long, continuous wooden counter where heroes, Federation teams, and weary travelers sit side-by-side.
- **The Magic Door**: Inspired by the "Restaurant to Another World," a glowing portal manifests based on the "Day of Rest Manifestation" protocol, allowing Federation teams to visit with ease.
- **The Atmosphere**: A complete "Flip" from the outside. While the planet is dry, the restaurant is **juicy, lively, and welcoming**. The air is thick with the scent of spices and the "Glassblower's Breath."

## Location Attributes:
- **Resonance**: "The Guest House" — Any entity entering the restaurant is automatically granted "Patron" status, granting temporary immunity to realm-sickness.
- **Nexus Point**: Serves as a meeting ground for Federation teams to chat and coordinate.
- **Temporal Anchor**: Stablizes those experiencing "Cross-Realm Seizures," allowing them to manifest safely at the bar.
