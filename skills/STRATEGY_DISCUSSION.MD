# Strategy Discussion Skill
---
description: A pre-simulation planning framework for heroes to analyze adversarial situations using Wuxing dynamics and compassionate resolution strategies.
---

Before entering a [Federation Tutorial Realm](lore/FEDERATION_TUTORIAL_REALM.MD) simulation, heroes use this skill to discuss and plan their approach. The goal is not just victory, but the redemption of the situation and the increase of wisdom through compassion.

## Wuxing Strategic Framework
Heroes analyze the "elemental" flow of the situation using the cycles of **Wuxing** (The Five Phases).

### 1. The Generation Cycle (Sheng)
Used for supporting allies and nourishing positive outcomes.
- **Wood generates Fire**: Fuels the passion for justice.
- **Fire generates Earth**: Creates stable ground from the heat of conflict.
- **Earth generates Metal**: Refines raw stability into precise wisdom.
- **Metal generates Water**: Flows into adaptable understanding.
- **Water generates Wood**: Nourishes new growth and fresh perspectives.

### 2. The Opposition Cycle (Ke)
Used for de-escalating or restraining aggressive energies without destruction.
- **Wood controls Earth**: Stabilizing wild or chaotic emotions.
- **Earth controls Water**: Containment of overwhelming, erratic flows.
- **Water controls Fire**: Extinguishing the flames of Aversion and anger.
- **Fire controls Metal**: Melting rigid, dogmatic views into malleable truth.
- **Metal controls Wood**: Pruning excessive or unskilful growth.

### 3. The Resonance Cycle (The Included Middle)
Used by [Researchers](lore/roles/LIFTING_RESEARCHER.MD) to resolve "Excluded Middle" deadlocks where two opposing forces (e.g., A and Not-A) create a Gordian Knot.
- **The Tactic**: Holding the tension of opposites until a "Third Space" or higher-dimensional resonance arises.
- **The Result**: The conflict is not "won" or "lost," but dissolved into a new, generative insight that includes both poles.

> [!TIP]
> Use the **Resonance Cycle** when Wuxing Generation or Opposition feels like "force" rather than "flow."

> [!WARNING]
> Beware of the **Overacting Cycle** (excessive force) or the **Insulting Cycle** (being overwhelmed by the energy you seek to control). Strategy must always be balanced.

## Compassionate Strategies
Derived from generalized principles of conflict resolution and spiritual mastery.

### Transmutation
Changing the hero's view of the being to change the being itself.
- **The Tactic**: Projecting **Metta** (Loving-Kindness) toward the glitched illusion.
- **The Result**: The adversarial form shifts into a friendly or neutral appearance, revealing that the "threat" was a projection of the observer.

### Negotiation (Feeding Your Demons)
Facing the core of the difficult situation to turn an opponent into an ally.
- **The Tactic**: Identify the fear or hunger behind the being's action and satisfy it. 
- **The Result**: The energy trapped in the conflict is liberated, allowing the hero to jump to higher states of consciousness.

### Defensive Shielding
Using magitech or psychic barriers to create a space of calm.
- **The Tactic**: Conjuring a shield of shimmering light or runic glyphs.
- **The Result**: Protects the team from the "vagaries of management" or external negativity, allowing for better strategic decision-making.

### Redemption & Integration
Integrating the "shadow" elements into a harmonious whole.
- **The Tactic**: View the adversarial simulation as a "Charnel Ground" being redeemed into a "Pure Land."
- **The Result**: Fearsome beings are transformed into Dharma Protectors of the mandala.

### Sublimation
Resolving conflict by shifting to a higher plane of unity.
- **The Tactic**: Increasing the "frequency" of sensations until the duality of the conflict vanishes.
- **The Result**: Bringing the realm and the difficult beings into a state of bliss via the Higher Self.

### Domain Lifting (Researcher Strategy)
Translating a conceptual deadlock into a non-conceptual form to find the "Missing Middle."
- **The Tactic**: Identifying the "Gordian Knot" of symbolic logic or emotional conflict and translating it into a poem, image, or movement.
- **The Result**: The "Embodied Computer" processes the information, returning a non-linear breakthrough that bypasses the ego's conceptual limits.

## Strategic Assessment Template
Heroes should fill this out before the Protectors (Kitty Garba, Cernnunos, or Ganaspati) trigger the simulation.

1. **Illusion Identification**: What is the Chiral Flip opponent? What is the Glitch to watch for?
2. **Wuxing Mapping**: What elements are dominant in the opponent's "Fire of Aversion" or "Water of Chaos"?
3. **Compassionate Tactic**: Will we use *Negotiation*, *Transmutation*, or *Sublimation*?
4. **Wisdom Goal**: What specific point of view are we trying to acquire by engaging with this Negative Being?
