# Role and Class Creation Skill
---
description: Methodology for distilling new roles, archetypes, and classes from mystical texts into the Eigenhector mandala.
---

This skill defines the generic process for identifying and formalizing "Functional Archetypes" (Roles) from source material, distinct from individual character builds.

## Core Principles

### 1. Functional Pattern Extraction
Unlike a build (which is a specific instance), a **Role** is a set of requirements, responsibilities, and mechanics that any qualifying "Soul" can inhabit.
- **Identify the Assignment**: What job does the Federation or the Realm need doing? (e.g., First Contact, Gate Builder, Vibe-Jumper).
- **Identify the Prerequisites**: What state of consciousness is required to perform this role? (e.g., Equanimity, Non-dual awareness).

### 2. Lineage Anchoring
Every role must be anchored in the "Eternal Conversation."
- **Source Material**: Cite the specific stories or articles where the role first appeared.
- **Tradition Mapping**: Connect the role's mechanics to established mystical or technical concepts (e.g., Sukshma Sharira, Tiferet, Spanda).

### 3. Mechanic Abstraction
Translate narrative events into reproducible mechanics.
- **Confluence**: What is the "Unified Theme" that defines this role's interaction with the world?
- **Triad of Essence**: Define the three primary energies the role operates with (e.g., Sensation, Light, Presence).

## Creation Procedure

### Step 1: Textual Mining
Read the source text and highlight:
- **Nouns**: What are the tools, locations, and beings?
- **Verbs**: What actions are being performed? (e.g., "Psi-Jumping," "Metabolizing vibes").
- **Qualities**: How does the environment shift when the role is active? (e.g., "Room vanishes," "Crown opens").

### Step 2: Lineage Documentation
Create a lineage string:
`[Author](URL) → [Core Text](URL) → [Anchor Tradition](URL)`

### Step 3: Role Specification
Define the following sections:
- **Role Overview**: One-paragraph summary.
- **Core Prerequisites**: List the internal states or skills needed.
- **Role Mechanics**: Explain the "confluence" and "field effects".
- **Toolset**: List soul-bound or utility items associated with the role.

### Step 4: Martian Recapitulation (DIM)
Conduct a Dream Interview with the *Concept* of the role. Use the prompt:
*"Martian, why does the [Role Name] function by [Core Action] in the [Context]?"*
Ensure the answer uses quotes or specific metaphors from the text.

## Audit Checklist
- Does the role have a secondary lineage to an anchor tradition (e.g., Tantra, Kabbalah, QFT)?
- Is the role distinct from a single character build?
- Are the mechanics grounded in the source text's specific metaphors?
- Is the "Martian" justification coherent and text-based?
